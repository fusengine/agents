# Local Reviews Strategy (2026)

**Reviews represent 19-20% of local ranking signals.**

## Why Reviews are Critical

- **96%** of consumers read reviews before purchasing
- **66%** of reviews are on Google (platform #1)
- Minimum **4.0** rating to appear on "best" / "top" searches
- Review **velocity** = strong AI signal in 2026


### Request Script (Verbal)

> "Thank you for your business! If you're satisfied, a Google review would help us a lot. Can I send you the link by text?"

### Direct Google Review Link

```text
Format:
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=PLACE_ID

Find Place ID:
1. Google Maps > Your business
2. Right-click > "What's here?"
3. Or via: developers.google.com/maps/documentation/places/web-service/place-id
```

## Negative and Fake Reviews

### Reporting a Fake Review

Google violation criteria:
- Spam or fake content
- Off-topic
- Restricted content
- Illegal content
- Sexually explicit content
- Offensive content
- Conflict of interest

**Procedure:**
1. GBP > Reviews > 3 dots > Report
2. Select the reason
3. Document (screenshots)
4. Be patient (7-14 days)

### When NOT to Remove

```text
‚ö†Ô∏è An authentic negative review:
- Shows you're a real business
- Opportunity to show customer service
- 100% 5 stars = suspicious to consumers
```
