# Google Ads Quality Score

**Improving ad relevance, CTR, and landing page experience.**

## Quality Score Factors

| Factor | Weight | How to Improve |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Expected CTR** | 40% | Compelling ad copy, extensions |
| **Ad Relevance** | 30% | Match ad to keyword |
| **Landing Page** | 30% | Fast, relevant, mobile-friendly |

---

## Score Range

- **1-3**: Poor (high CPC)
- **4-6**: Average
- **7-10**: Excellent (low CPC, better position)

---

## Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Ad copy includes keyword
- [ ] Landing page matches ad promise
- [ ] Page speed <3 seconds
- [ ] Mobile-responsive
- [ ] Clear CTA above fold
- [ ] Trust signals (reviews, certifications)
