# Landing Page Optimization (Google Ads)

**Converting ad clicks to conversions.**

## Requirements

1. **Headline matches ad**: If ad says "Free SEO Audit", headline should too
2. **CTA above fold**: Visible without scrolling
3. **Fast loading**: <3 seconds
4. **Mobile-responsive**: 50%+ traffic is mobile
5. **Trust signals**: Reviews, logos, certifications

---

## Template Structure

```html
<header>
  <h1>Get Your Free SEO Audit</h1> <!-- Matches ad -->
  <p>Discover ranking opportunities in 60 seconds</p>
  <button>Start Free Audit</button> <!-- CTA above fold -->
</header>

<section>
  <h2>What You'll Get</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Technical SEO analysis</li>
    <li>Keyword opportunities</li>
    <li>Competitor comparison</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>Trusted by 10,000+ Businesses</h2>
  <img src="client-logos.jpg" alt="Client logos">
</section>

<footer>
  <button>Get Free Audit Now</button> <!-- Repeat CTA -->
</footer>
```
