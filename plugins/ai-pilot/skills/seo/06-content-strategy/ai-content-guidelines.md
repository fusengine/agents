# AI-Generated Content Guidelines (2026)

**Google's stance on AI content (2025 update).**

## Google's Position

✅ **AI content is acceptable** IF it provides value
❌ **Scaled content abuse** is penalized

---

## Quality Requirements

### ✅ Acceptable AI Use

```markdown
1. Human review and editing
2. Adds unique value/insights
3. E-E-A-T signals present
4. Accurate, up-to-date
5. Cited sources
```

### ❌ Spam (Penalized)

```markdown
1. Mass-generated without review
2. Thin, low-value content
3. Keyword stuffing
4. No authorship/expertise
5. Auto-published at scale
```

---

## Best Practices 2026

1. **Human-in-Loop**: Always review AI output
2. **Add Experience**: Personal insights, examples
3. **Fact-Check**: Verify claims, add sources
4. **Update Regularly**: AI training data is dated
5. **Disclose**: Optional transparency (some brands do)

---

## E-E-A-T for AI Content

```markdown
# Add human expertise

AI Draft:
"Core Web Vitals are important for SEO."

Human Edit:
"Based on my analysis of 500 client sites, Core Web Vitals impact rankings by ~15-20%. Sites with LCP <2.5s rank average 2.3 positions higher (case study: example.com increased traffic 45% after CWV optimization)."
```
