# Citation Strategies (Princeton 10 Tactics)

**Research-backed GEO optimization techniques (30-40% visibility increase).**

## Princeton University Research 2025

10 proven tactics to increase AI citations:

### 1. Cite Authoritative Sources (40% boost)

```markdown
❌ "SEO is important for businesses."

✅ "According to Moz's State of SEO 2025 report, 61% of marketers say SEO is their top priority (Source: Moz, 2025)."
```

**Implementation**:
- Link to academic research, industry reports
- Mention publication name + year
- Use inline citations: (Source: Name, Year)

---

### 2. Include Statistics (40% boost)

```markdown
❌ "Most searches are zero-click."

✅ "60% of Google searches result in zero clicks, meaning users get answers without visiting a website (SparkToro, 2025)."
```

**Best Practices**:
- Use specific numbers (not "many", "most")
- Include source and year
- Update regularly (outdated stats hurt credibility)

---

### 3. Add Expert Quotes (30% boost)

```markdown
❌ "E-E-A-T is important."

✅ "As Google Search Liaison Danny Sullivan stated: 'E-E-A-T is how we evaluate whether our systems are showing helpful, reliable information' (Google, 2025)."
```

**Guidelines**:
- Quote recognized experts
- Include full attribution (name, title, organization)
- Use quotation marks

---

### 4. Layered Content Structure (25% boost)

```markdown
# How to Optimize Core Web Vitals

**Quick Answer**: Optimize images, minimize JavaScript, and use a CDN to achieve LCP <2.5s, INP <200ms, and CLS <0.1.

## Detailed Explanation
[2-3 paragraphs with more context...]

## Step-by-Step Process
[Detailed implementation...]

## Advanced Techniques
[Expert-level optimization...]
```

**Structure**: Simple → Detailed → Nuanced

---

### 5. Standalone Paragraphs (20% boost)

```markdown
❌ Long paragraph mixing multiple ideas without clear topic sentences.

✅ E-E-A-T stands for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trust. It's Google's framework for evaluating content quality.

✅ Each factor has specific signals. Experience means first-hand knowledge. Expertise requires subject matter authority.
```

**Rules**:
- One idea per paragraph
- 2-4 sentences max
- Can be quoted independently
- Clear topic sentence

### 10. Recency Signals (15% boost)

```markdown
✅ "As of January 2026, Google AI Overviews appear on 16% of queries (Search Engine Land, 2026)."

❌ "Google recently updated..." (no date)
```

**Recency Indicators**:
- Publication date visible
- "Updated: [Date]" label
- Current year in examples
- Recent sources cited
